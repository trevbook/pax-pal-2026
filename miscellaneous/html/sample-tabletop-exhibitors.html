TABLETOP EXPO HALL

If you’ve ever been to PAX, you know that tabletop is a HUGE part of PAX, to the extent that it’s almost a show on its own (ahem ahem PAX Unplugged). It’s so big, and we have so many participating tabletop exhibitors, it seemed silly for us not to make it easier for people to find those tabletop exhibitors in the sea of video games.
